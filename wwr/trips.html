Merced River

After plunging more than 1,400 feet over Yosemite Falls and winding gently through Yosemite Valley, the Merced River still has something left for us, namely: great whitewater.

Sierra Mac River

Sierra Mac River Rafting Trips specializes in Yosemite rafting trips. They were the first outfitter on the Merced River and the pioneer in opening Class V Cherry Creek to commercial rafting.

Scout's Landing

After many troups began using our rives for activites and merit badge collecting, we set out to help streamline the fun directly to your troup. We offer a full BOA certified camp to help you get your next Scouting rank!
